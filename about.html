About Us

"Welcome to Cockfosters Carpentry, where woodworking is not just a trade; it's an art form. Since our establishment in 1980, we have been devoted to the timeless craft of carpentry, breathing life into your ideas and transforming spaces with our bespoke creations. With a team of skilled artisans, we bring an unwavering commitment to excellence, precision, and the inherent beauty of wood.

Our journey is rooted in a deep passion for craftsmanship, from the careful selection of materials to the meticulous execution of each project. Whether you envision custom furniture, unique cabinetry, or intricate woodworking solutions, we collaborate closely with our clients, ensuring that every piece reflects their individual style and needs.

At Cockfosters Carpentry, we believe in creating not just furniture, but enduring works of art. Our dedication to quality is evident in every detail, and we take pride in the seamless fusion of traditional techniques with contemporary design.

As craftsmen who understand the language of wood, we invite you to explore the artistry and precision that define our work. From the warmth of natural finishes to the sophistication of tailored designs, let Cockfosters Carpentry be your partner in turning your space into a true reflection of your taste and lifestyle.

Thank you for considering us. We look forward to the opportunity of bringing the richness of woodworking into your life."
